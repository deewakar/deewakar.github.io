10 Rules to live life
=====================

#. Don't wish things in life rather act to achive things
#. Don't make promises you can't fulfill
#. Don't hide your love
#. Don't hold onto bitter feelings
#. Don't ignore your intuition
#. Don't hang onto regrets
#. Don't get stuck i.e. in a rut (bad situation will pass, work on being
   unstuck)
#. Don't stop educating yourself
#. Don't lose your individuality (what makes you unique?)
#. Don't expect your children to give back what you gave to them
